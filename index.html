FAQ

Where do I find my FPL Team ID?
Log in at fantasy.premierleague.com, go to the "Points" tab, and check the number in the URL after /entry/.

Why am I not receiving notifications?
Make sure notifications are enabled in both the app settings and your device's system settings.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account" at the bottom.
